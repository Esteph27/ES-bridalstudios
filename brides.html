“A dress that says something more”

Paige

23.05.21

Her story


Boutique after boutique, Paige had tried on the same glitzy, mermaidy, expensive dress that left her feeling uncomfortable. She came to me hoping for an “aha” moment.

Wanting a contemporary, feminine yet demure dress, she dreaded having to wear something she wouldn’t normally, for the sake of the big day. She wanted to feel relaxed and herself.

Together, we achieved this stunning look with it’s subtle nod to Paige’s hippie side, while still classy and ethereal.
