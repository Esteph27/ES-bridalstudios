Questions

Q: I’m not sure after the consultation. What do I do?

Don’t worry! The consultation is non-obligatory. You are more than welcome to sleep on it or to say then and there that, upon reflection, you’d like to use a different service. We understand. It has to feel right.

Q: How many fittings will I need?

This often varies, but at least 2-3.

Q: When it’s ready, how do I collect my dress?

You’re free to take your dress home after the final fitting, pick it up from the studio another day, or, have it delivered to your home via a secure courier which is always on us.

Q: Where is the studio?

We’re in East London, in the Royal Docks (please see our contact page for our address).

Q: How much does everything cost?

Prices start from £2,500. We can discuss your budget in the consulation to inform fabric and design decisions.

Q: How long does it take?

This also varies but between 6-10 months.

Q: What fabrics do you use?

We only work with the highest quality fabrics such as 100% silk. Regarding any skin intolerances, let’s discuss options in your consulation!

Q: I don’t want to pay £80 for a consultation, can I skip it?

Unfortunately, not. It’s very important that you make an informed decision when choosing a studio. Designing a wedding dress is a journey, and the consulation is crucial for a successful, enjoyable one.

Q: What is your refund policy?

As every dress is made to your exact requirements, they are non refundable, However, since you’re there for every stage of the design process, there are no surprises.
